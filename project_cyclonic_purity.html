Design Concept 設計理念

This project focuses on the core principles of cyclonic separation to deliver a pure and efficient coffee roasting experience. 本專案著重於旋風分離的核心原理，旨在提供純淨且高效的烘豆體驗。

By optimizing airflow and structural components, we achieved a balance of form and function suitable for artisanal craftsmanship. 透過優化氣流與結構組件，我們實現了適合職人工藝的形式與功能平衡。
