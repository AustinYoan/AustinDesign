About Me 關於我

I am a Product Designer with a unique foundation in metal processing and industrial craftsmanship. My journey began in the workshop, where I learned the tangible properties of materials—the resistance of steel, the malleability of aluminum, and the precision required to shape them. 我是一位產品設計師，擁有紮根於金屬加工與工業工藝的獨特背景。我的設計旅程始於工廠車間，在那裡我深刻體悟了材料的實質特性——鋼鐵的剛毅抗性、鋁材的柔韌延展，以及將其成型所需的精準度。

This hands-on experience with raw materials has profoundly influenced my design philosophy. I don't just design for aesthetics; I design for structural integrity, tactile experience, and manufacturability. I believe that true innovation lies at the intersection of artistic vision and engineering reality. 這段親手處理原物料的實作經驗，深深影響了我的設計哲學。我的設計不單是為了追求美學，更是為了結構的完整性、觸覺的體驗感，以及產品的可製造性。我堅信，真正的創新，誕生於藝術視野與工程現實的交會之處。
