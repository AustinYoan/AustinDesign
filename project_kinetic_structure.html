Kinetic Structure 動態結構：城市滑軌遊具

Public Design & Safety 公共設計與安全

Client 客戶 City Council 市政府
Date 年份 2024
Role 擔任角色 Lead Designer 首席設計師
Material 主要材質 Steel & Polymer 鋼材與聚合物

It had to work on the first try. 第一次就要能溜完

A 20-meter zipline through a public park. The brief was simple. The engineering wasn't. 一條穿過公園的 20 米滑軌。要求聽起來很簡單，工程上不是。

The trolley couldn't get stuck. 台車不能卡住

Before anything was manufactured, I ran full motion analysis and structural load calculations to meet safety regulations — working through the energy conversion precisely enough that the trolley would complete the full run regardless of rider weight or start position. First production, first ride, no stalling. 在任何東西開始製造之前，我先做了完整的運動分析和靜動態承重的法規結構計算——把位能轉動能的過程算得足夠精準，讓台車不管乘客體重或起始位置，都能順利溜完全程。第一次製造，第一次乘坐，沒有卡頓。

Taiwan has typhoons. 台灣有颱風

Wind load was another layer — CFD simulation under Category 16 gusts, truss geometry optimized to redirect pressure rather than absorb it. The structure passed. 風載是另一個課題——用 CFD 模擬 16 級陣風，調整桁架幾何讓風壓有地方分散而不是硬抗。結構通過了。

It ran exactly as designed from day one. 從第一天開始就照設計在跑

It held up well for a good while — outdoor wear and some human impact eventually took a toll, as they always do with public installations. But the engineering delivered what it promised. 裝好之後運作得很好——戶外環境和使用耗損是公共設施的現實，後來也有些損毀。但工程本身完全達到了當初承諾的目標。
